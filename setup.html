Your Calculator

Setting up the nspire-ti calculator is easy for most users. You either need a physical calculator or the nspire software. The Ti-nspire student software is a power math software that allows you to run the calculator on your computer. Learn more about the student software on the TI-nspire resource page.

Setting up your calculator

1) Download the TI-snpire software
2) Open the program and install
3a) Open the app
3b) On intial launch, you will need to either activate your license or purchase one. You may use a 30 day trial if you do not already own the software.

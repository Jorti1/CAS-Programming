TI-Basic Programming

Adapted for TI-nspire CX II CAS

Before starting...

In this guide we will begin with the most basic instructions. I have not previously made any basic programs; however, I have some experience in other programming languages. We will assume you know absolutly nothing about programming.

Programming can be very frustrating at times. Remember to take a break and step outside. The new TI-nspire CX II CAS, released in 2019, is drarastically different from previous models. Even if you have previously programmed other calculators, such as the TI-84 plus, you will struggle and we encourage you to wipe your tears and persevere. No, seriously, it will get difficult.
